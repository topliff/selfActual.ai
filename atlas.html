The Problem

You take a strengths assessment at work. A personality test online. A coaching program gives you a report. Each one lives in a different app, a different PDF, a different inbox. None of them talk to each other. And none of them remember what the others said.

What Atlas Does

One place for every assessment. One AI that understands all of them.

Atlas is a self-knowledge navigator. It collects your assessment results from any source — structured intake forms, PDF uploads, partner integrations — and gives you an AI coach that can see the whole picture at once.

Bring everything together.

StrengthsFinder. DISC. Star Card. Enneagram. Flow assessments. Whatever tools you've used — Atlas can take the data in. Structured forms for common assessments, AI extraction for PDFs and reports you already have.

No more hunting through emails for that one PDF from a workshop two years ago. Your results live in one place, structured and linked.

Assessment intake preview

AI that sees the patterns.

Each assessment tells you something. Atlas's AI coach sees the connections between them — where your StrengthsFinder results align with your Star Card, where your flow attributes explain your work patterns, what it all means together.

This isn't generic coaching. It's grounded in your actual data — specific scores, specific dimensions, specific patterns that are yours.

Data that lasts.

Most assessment platforms lock your results in their app. If you stop paying, you lose access to your own data. Atlas writes your results to your personal vault — they're yours regardless of which tools you use.

Take an assessment through AllStarTeams, import a PDF from a coaching program, manually enter your Enneagram type — it all ends up in the same place.
