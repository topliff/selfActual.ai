The Foundation

One vault. Every app connected.

Your vault is the personal data store at the center of SelfActual. Every app reads from it and writes to it — which means your Imprint profile, your Atlas assessments, and your Prompter reflections all enrich each other automatically.

🔒

Private by default

Your vault belongs to you. Reflections, session logs, and personal observations stay private unless you explicitly choose to share them.

🔗

Apps talk to each other

When Atlas adds an assessment, Imprint can reference it in coaching. When Prompter captures a reflection, it deepens the AI's understanding of you across every app.

🧳

Portable forever

Your data is stored in open, standard formats. If you leave SelfActual, your vault — and everything in it — comes with you. No lock-in. No data hostage.
